## As the Bouncer

### I Will Start With Clarity
When I take 2-3 mins to start with one clear sentence, I help myself and my Sounder to make the most of this convo.

### I Trust The Process
When I share my starting sentence and stop, I know this starting point is enough. My Sounder's curiosity will guide me from here.

### Curiosity is Valuable Currency
When I let my Sounder's curiosity guide me, I gain the clarity, insight, and action step I need to move in the direction of my goals. This is very valuable.

---

## As the Sounder

### My Curiosity is Rare and Valuable
We're taught to believe that we give 'value' when we give advice, suggestions, solutions, etc.

When I'm a great listener and ask the questions I most want to hear the answer to, this is rare and valuable for my Bouncer.

### I Trust The Process
When I trust the process, my Bouncer gains the clarity, insight, and action step they need to move in the direction of their goals.

### The 10 min Post-Bounce Buffer Gives Us a Little More Time
- Is there a resource you think your partner would benefit from?
- Do you have a perspective and/or feedback your partner might benefit from?
- Was there a question you didn't have time to ask?

*Ask for their interest / permission / consent first.*
